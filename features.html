Features

Learn about some of the ways we protect your privacy.

Privacy by Design

Goodbye, Google

📲 Download your favorite Android apps from the Aurora and F-Droid Stores.

🙌 Removed dependencies from Google APIs using microG.

👍 Verified Boot ensures authenticity of CalyxOS.

Intelligent Privacy

⏰ Automatically disable bluetooth when not in use.

🚨 Wipe your device in an emergency situation.

You're in control

✋ Control which apps connect to the internet with Datura Firewall.

🔐 Use built-in VPNs from Calyx and Riseup to secure and anonymize your traffic.
